## 🧱 FORGE Framework

My PhD research is unified under **FORGE** — *Framework for Out-of-distribution Robustness, Generalisation and Evaluation* — a systematic programme for stress-testing federated and multimodal AI systems under real-world distribution shift.

| Component | Full Name | Focus |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| **FORGE-BENCH** | MedFL-Stress | Robustness benchmarking of federated medical imaging under cross-site heterogeneity |
| **FORGE-DIAG** | DAMF | Diagnosing multimodal instability in vision-language models under severe physical domain shift |
| **FORGE-EVAL** | Worst-Client FL NLP | Evaluating worst-client robustness in federated text classification under Non-IID shift |
| **FORGE-ADAPT** | Adaptive-DAMF | Gradient-signal stage switching for adaptive robustness in fine-tuning pipelines *(in progress)* |
